About Our Restaurant

Welcome to the Jordanian Traditional Restaurant, where we serve authentic Jordanian cuisine in a warm and inviting atmosphere. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients to create delicious dishes that capture the rich flavors of Jordanian culture. Whether you're here for a family dinner, a special occasion, or just to enjoy a great meal, we strive to make your experience memorable.

Our Specialties

  • Mansaf - A traditional Jordanian dish made with lamb, rice, and a special yogurt sauce.
  • Falafel - Delicious deep-fried balls made from ground chickpeas and spices.
  • Makloubeh - A flavorful layered dish of rice, vegetables, and meat, turned upside down when served.
  • Knafeh - A popular dessert made with thin noodle-like pastry, soaked in sweet syrup, and layered with cheese or cream.
